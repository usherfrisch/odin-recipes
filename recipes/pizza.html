In the recipe here, you will find what i descrobe as a Fresh Home madePizza.

Suitable for any occasion like a family luch or dinner!

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Fresh Yeast
  • Salt
  • Corn Meal

Steps:

  1. Pour Water into flour
  2. Put in yeast
  3. Mix it for a min
  4. Put Salt in
  5. Finish mixing until firm
