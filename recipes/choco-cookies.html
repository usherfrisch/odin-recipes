Chocholate Chip Cookie Recipe:

Click Here To navigate back to the Main page.

In the recipe here you find what i describe as a comfort home made Chocholate Chip cookie.

Tha is suitable for any occasion especially for just munching when the craving is there for a quick bite!

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Choco Chips
  • Water
  • Suger
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Suger
  • Fresh Brewed Coffe

Steps:

  1. Pour flour into bowl
  2. Pour Chocholate chps into bowl
  3. pour Vanilla Suger into bowl
  4. Mix Well
  5. pour all the wet Ingredients into bowl
  6. mix until firm
  7. make 8oz bowls
  8. Flatten onto cookie sheet
  9. back for 17 minuts on 350
